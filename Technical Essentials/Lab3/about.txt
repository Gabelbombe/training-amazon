AWS Technical Essentials - Lab 3 - Amazon ELB

Lab Description: Elastic Load Balancing is used to distribute incoming application traffic across multiple Amazon EC2 instances. It enables you to achieve even greater fault tolerance in your 
applications, seamlessly providing the amount of load balancing capacity needed in response to incoming application traffic. In this lab you will create an elastic load balancer for HTTP traffic.
